PGR (Punishing: Gray Raven/战双帕弥什) is one of the most popular ARPG gacha games due to its abundance of explicit and implicit/non-obvious in-game mechanics. Explicit mechanics: 1.Main orbs are only: red, yellow, blue stacking up to 16. 2.Some characters may have special orb that can be obtained through core passives. 3.Some characters may have timestop mechanic that makes enemies freezed during cast. (Exception: specific in-game modes) 4.Matrix is the system that makes enimies slowed down, but there're also cases when matrix doesn't affect certain enemy types. 5.QTE is available to cast in case of pinging 3 orbs or pinging any amount of orbs during matrix. 6.Damages of any matrix orbs pinged and 3 orbs are the same. (Exception: specific buff in roguelike modes) 7.Different characters may have different QTE cast duration. 8.Gear related: characters may require different builds depending on overall team invest and target content. Non-obvious mechanics: 1.Dodge allows to cancel some character animations. 2.After character swap, newly entered character will recieve SP points. 4.Choosing different initial orbs may affect to clear time. 5.Physical damage has it's own formula and this considered as non-elemental attribute/factor. 6.Physical damage has its own random spread factor, so result may differ from 95% to 105% physical damage output. 7.Elemental damage has its own fixed value. 8.Elemental damage can't crit. 8.Elemental damage is unaffected by DEF Stats and Armor. 9.While dealing affix damage some characters may also trigger its own elemental damage, so elemental damage instances may significantly differ. 10.SP gauge fills in case of pressing orb and signature move simultaneously. 11.More damage output in case of activating 4 piece DV effect at the last possible frame. 12.Damage may differ positively if player pings non DV-carry character in order to ping DV-carry to make its 4 piece set bonus effect activate first pinged character. 13.Some characters allow to buffer input. (Buffer input allows to ping orbs faster than usual)
